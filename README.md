# 📊 Customer Insights & Recommendation Project

## Data Analytics using Python, SQL & Power BI

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The Customer Insights & Recommendation Project is an end-to-end Data Analytics project that analyzes customer purchasing behavior, sales performance, product profitability, and regional trends using Python, SQL, and Power BI.

The objective is to transform raw business data into meaningful insights and provide actionable recommendations that help improve customer retention, increase profitability, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify high-value customers
- Evaluate sales performance
- Analyze product performance
- Study regional sales trends
- Perform customer segmentation
- Generate business insights
- Provide business recommendations

## 💡 Business Insights

- High-value customers contribute significantly to total revenue.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies across regions.
- Some products generate high sales but low profit.
- Seasonal trends influence customer demand.
- Digital payment methods are becoming more popular.
- Regional performance differs significantly.
- Customer segmentation helps improve marketing strategies.
- Profit margin should be monitored along with sales.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Launch loyalty programs for high-value customers.
- Increase inventory for top-selling products.
- Improve pricing strategies for low-profit products.
- Run targeted campaigns in underperforming regions.
- Promote digital payment methods.
- Personalize offers using customer segmentation.
- Improve customer retention strategies.
- Forecast seasonal demand using historical sales data.
